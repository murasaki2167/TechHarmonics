INFORMATION

Why did we decide to make this piano? What's our aim?

This touch sensor piano, named the WaveSync Piano, was created specifically those with hand impairments and kids whose hands aren't as flexible as others. It's also beneficial for kids who are just starting to learn about music. We recognized the importance of creating a piano that would cater to one's unique needs and allow them to explore their musical talents without limitations. By incorporating touch sensor technology, we aim to offer a user-friendly and intuitive instrument that enables effortless playing, fostering creativity and enjoyment for all users. Our ultimate goal is to promote inclusivity, ignite a love for music, and empower individuals to express themselves through the joy of playing the WaveSync Piano.
